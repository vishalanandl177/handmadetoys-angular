Placing an order

In-stock Collections: At most times, the products from our current catalog, will be in stock and ready to ship. Occasionally, because people love our products, some of our product inventory might be temporarily sold out. We encourage you to call our studio, or write us an email to check the current availability of our in stock collections. If the products from our in stock collection are not available, or if you need more quantity than we have with us, you can place an order with us for the same. Due to the handcrafted nature of our products, we may need some time to make them prepare again, and then get our finished pieces on their way to you. To place a order, write to us at mandmadetoyz4u@gmail.com.

Returns and Cancellations

We care a great deal about customer satisfaction and the quality of our products. Each piece that leaves our Studio is inspected by hand to ensure the same. All are our products are hand made.Variation and little differences from sample website pic is inherent due to the hand crafted nature of our pieces . We feel that these characteristics add to the natural beauty and unique quality of our products and are in no way flaws.

Terms and Conditions for Returns:

• As a policy, we do not offer returns or exchanges on products which are delivered in perfect condition as per the order placed.
• The dimensions, measurements and appearance of the products which are listed on this website are to the best of our approximations and any challenges regarding the same will not be entertained.
• In the event of an incorrect order, being delivered please notify us within 24 hours via email at mandmadetoyz4u@gmail.com. We will arrange for the package to be picked up at the earliest from the delivery address provided while placing the order. The package must be returned in its original condition and packaging; complete with all tags, instructions, and inserts. We will send you your order within 24 hours of receiving the package with the incorrect order.
• Any products returned without all original packaging and accessories, including the retail box, manuals, inserts, and all other items originally included with the product at the time of delivery will not be replaced.
• If you notice that the packaging is tampered with or damaged before delivery, please refuse to accept the package and return the package to the delivery person.
• If you notice that your product is damaged after opening the package, please take a photograph of the products along with their packaging and send it us within 24 hours of the package being received, to mandmadetoyz4u@gmail.com with the Subject - ‘Damaged Package’. Please mention your order number and date of transaction in the email.
• In case your product has been damaged in transit or has genuine quality/manufacturing defect,we are open to extending a store credit or re-fulfilling your order.
• As most of our pieces are of limited quantity or one-off pieces we may not be able to offer you an identical replacement. Instead, we will offer you a similar product and if that does not meet your approval, we will give you store credits for any future purchase (once the product has been shipped back to us in the condition it was received in). Please make sure that the original product tag and packing is intact.
• The store credit will be applicable for 6 months.
• Given the nature of our products, we have the sole discretion, to provide the resolution to any situation as we deem fit. Each return or exchange request is handled on a case by case basis and we request you to get in touch with our team for prompt resolution.
• No refunds would be given if the customer has provided wrong or incomplete shipping address, there are 3 failed delivery attempts by our shipping agency and/or the package is refused by the recipient.
• The Definition of a damaged product: A chipped, cracked or completely broken article is considered damaged. Variation in size, colour and fabric do not consider as damages.

• Once we receive your order, it will be processed within 4 business days.

• Delivery time is also subjective to delays arising out of bad weather conditions, strikes, public holidays or any other unexpected delay from our courier and travel partners.
• Your shipping costs are subject to the cost of the purchase being made. For any queries regarding the shipment of your purchase, please email us at mandmadetoyz4u@gmail.com.
